Shaders and Optifine

Shaders and Optifine are not officially supported. Use at your own risk.

That being said, use the absolutely latest version of Optifine. I recommend Complementary shaders in compatibility mode.

  • Shader config changes checklist
  • If Immersive Engineering is crashing when loading into a map with shaders, try loading into an empty world first.
