ET Upgrades
  • Frequency - speeds up miners
  • Bandwidth - increases amount of mined items
  • Amplification - is new Accuracy Modifier for lenses.
  • Piezo - allows solars to gen in rain/thunderstorms from vibrations.
  • Electrostatic - allows Lightning rods to gen passively in rain and thunder.
  • Radiant - allows lightning rod to gen passively in sunny weather.

Void Miner
